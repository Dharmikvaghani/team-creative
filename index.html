Introduction

Space technology has made significant strides over the past few decades, but numerous challenges remain. This website explores these challenges and the innovative solutions being developed to overcome them.

Challenges

  • High costs of space missions
  • Long-duration space travel effects on human health
  • Space debris and collision risks
  • Reliability of spacecraft systems

Solutions

Scientists and engineers are working on various solutions to address these challenges:

  • Developing cost-effective launch technologies
  • Enhancing life support systems for long-duration missions
  • Implementing debris mitigation strategies
  • Advancing spacecraft reliability through rigorous testing
